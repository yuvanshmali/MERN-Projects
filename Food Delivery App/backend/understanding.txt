What is multer?
Multer = middleware for handling file uploads in Express

Browser sends form-data (not JSON), Normal Express cannot read files, Multer reads the file, saves it, and gives info to your backend
Think like this: Express understands text, Multer teaches Express how to understand files

You need Multer when your frontend sends:
Images, PDFs, Videos, Any file via <input type="file" />
